Flexbox
- one dimensional layout model
- i.e. either row or column

Main-axis: defined by flex-direction
- row, row-reverse, column, column-reverse
Cross-axis: perpendicular

*axis are the key to aligning and justifying flex items

=========
flex-container
- area of document laid out using flexbox
- items do not stretch on main dimension, but can shrink
- items stretch to fill size of cross axis
- flex-basis: default to auto
- flex-wrap: defaults to nowrap
